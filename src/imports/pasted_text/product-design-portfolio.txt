## 3. VISUAL STYLE

Create a premium product design portfolio, not a decorative Behance poster.

Visual direction:

- clean
- minimal
- high-end
- structured
- generous whitespace
- strong grid
- modern SaaS/product aesthetic
- strong visual hierarchy
- calm confidence
- suitable for Munich / German / international product companies

Avoid:

- overly decorative gradients
- fake 3D objects
- loud colors
- childish illustrations
- huge walls of text
- generic design thinking diagrams
- fake numbers
- visual clutter
- too many different styles

Recommended typography:

Use a clean modern sans-serif such as:

- Inter
- Satoshi
- Manrope
- Neue Haas Grotesk style

Typography system:

- H1: 64–76 px
- H2: 40–48 px
- H3: 28–32 px
- Body: 20–24 px
- Small captions: 16–18 px
- Line height: 125–145%

Color system:

- Background: #F8F9FB or #FFFFFF
- Primary text: #111827
- Secondary text: #4B5563
- Muted text: #6B7280
- Border: #E5E7EB
- Accent: #2563EB or #2A6EF3
- Dark section background: #0F172A
- Light card background: #FFFFFF
- Subtle surface: #F3F4F6

Accessibility:

- Maintain strong contrast
- Do not place small text on busy images
- Use color + label, not color alone
- Keep spacing clear
- Use readable sizes for PDF
